2:-Malhargad Fort Chalisgaon


Malhargadh is the place situated in center of Kannad Ghat. Which is Recently Graded as Castle by Government Of Maharashtra. Nice place to visit. there is Temple too at this fort and surrounded by whole Sahyadri Mountain Range.

Good and calm place. One must visit the place to get refresh. Avoid summer time. You may go with your family and friends. Keep food and water with you. Respect Nature.
